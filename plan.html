Essential Information

Location

Kandy, Sri Lanka - Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic

Dates

July/August 2025 (Exact dates TBD)

Duration

10 nights of processions

Best Time to Arrive

4:00 PM to secure good viewing spots

Travel Tips

  • • Book accommodation well in advance
  • • Wear comfortable clothing and shoes
  • • Bring water and snacks
  • • Carry a good camera
  • • Respect local customs and dress codes
  • • Be prepared for crowds

Accommodation

We recommend booking your stay in Kandy city center, within walking distance of the parade route. Popular areas include:

  • • Kandy Lake Area
  • • Temple of the Tooth Vicinity
  • • Dalada Veediya (Main Street)
